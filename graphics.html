Evaluation of Web Graphics

Site evaluated Nike

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on this pages catches the attention of many users, mainly young adults. This makes the page look attractive and the first thing that catches your attention is the movement of the video being played. As you scroll down every image is a link and the images are layed out nicely. The simplicity of the graphics help the page look uniformed.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes- Graphics fit in with the purpose, organization, and style of the page
  2. Yes- Large graphics were avoided
  3. Yes- Graphics help guide the viewers' focus to the important content on the page
  4. Yes- Graphics with overly bright or obnoxious images have been avoided
  5. Yes- Grpahics were avoided to convey texual content
  6. Yes- Texual equivalent alternatives are provided for graphic content
  7. Yes- Graphics have a significant contrast between the text and background color
